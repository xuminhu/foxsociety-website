## The Origin of Fox Society

### The Fable & Yale President's Speech

Yale University President Peter Salovey encouraged students to emulate the fox in his 2017 freshman address.

The "Fox and Hedgehog" story originated from ancient Greek poets:
- **Hedgehog** sticks to one universal principle
- **Fox** finds connections between seemingly unrelated things

President Salovey said:

> "At university, I want to encourage you to be more like foxes, to learn different ideas and consider different perspectives. Try everything, then decide what suits you best."

## Our Mission

This is also the founding mission of Fox Society. We not only provide quality international education services but also focus on guiding students to:

1. **Open-mindedness** - Learn different perspectives and embrace diversity
2. **Independent Thinking** - Develop critical thinking skills
3. **Global Vision** - Become internationally-minded young scholars
